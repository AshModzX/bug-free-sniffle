Our Story

Rooted in tradition, crafted with pride

A Legacy of Handcrafted Footwear

JuttiDot.com was born from a deep love for South Asian artisanal tradition. Nestled in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal, our store celebrates the centuries-old art of Jutti and Kolhapuri footwear — each pair a tiny masterpiece of needle, thread, and leather.

We work directly with master craftsmen who have honed their skills over generations. Every stitch is placed by hand, every piece of leather hand-selected for quality. From the embroidered silk surface to the supple buffalo-leather sole, nothing is rushed or mass-produced.

Our collection spans everyday Juttis for women and men, festive pieces for weddings and celebrations, and a growing selection of accessories — all united by an uncompromising commitment to authenticity.
